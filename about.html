Heaven Voice Singers (HVS Choir) is a gospel music ministry based at AICT Mbezi Luis in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We are a passionate group of believers using the gift of music to glorify God and uplift souls through powerful praise and worship.

History

Founded with the vision of spreading the gospel through song, HVS Choir began as a small church group and has grown into a recognized name in gospel music. Over the years, we have produced inspiring songs and ministered in various church and public events across the country.

Our mission is to lead people into the presence of God through worship and to be a vessel of hope and encouragement. Our vision is to impact lives globally with spirit-filled music that transforms hearts and strengthens faith.

HVS MISSION

The mission of HVS Choir, under the spiritual guidance of AICT Tanzania, is to glorify God through spirit-filled worship and music ministry. The choir is committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ by singing songs rooted in biblical truth, uplifting both the congregation and the wider community. Through disciplined practice and heartfelt praise, HVS Choir serves as a vessel to encourage faith, nurture spiritual growth, and bring people closer to Christ.

HVS VISION

The vision of HVS Choir is to be a spiritually empowered and musically excellent ministry that transforms lives through worship. Aligned with the vision of AICT Tanzania, the choir seeks to reach beyond the church walls—evangelizing through music, inspiring believers, and drawing non-believers to the saving knowledge of Jesus. The choir envisions becoming a model of Christian service, unity, and devotion in both local and national platforms.

FOCUS.

HVS Choir, under the guidance of AICT Tanzania, exists to bring people to Jesus Christ through spirit-filled worship and Gospel-centered music. By using their voices as instruments of praise and evangelism, the choir seeks to touch hearts, inspire faith, and lead both believers and non-believers into a deeper relationship with Christ. Through every song, they aim to glorify God and share His love with the world..

We compose and perform gospel music, lead worship services, and engage in outreach through our music ministry. Our songs, such as “Walete Madhabahuni” and “Mzee wa Siku,” have touched many and continue to spread God’s message far and wide.

WORSHIP

HVS Choir serves as a vital part of the worship ministry within AICT Tanzania, leading praise and worship in church services, conferences, and spiritual gatherings. Through biblically grounded songs and prayerful preparation, the choir creates a worship environment where the Holy Spirit moves, and people are drawn closer to Jesus Christ. Our music reflects the teachings and spiritual focus of AICT, aiming to glorify God and edify the Church.

EVANGELIST

In line with AICT Tanzania’s evangelistic mission, HVS Choir actively participates in outreach programs, using music as a tool to share the Gospel beyond the church walls. We minister in villages, hospitals, schools, and open-air crusades—bringing the message of salvation, hope, and healing to all people, especially those who have not yet encountered Christ. Every song we sing is a testimony of God’s love and a call to repentance and faith..

FUNDRAISING EVENTS

As part of our ministry and service under AICT Tanzania, HVS Choir also organizes fundraising events to support the choir’s activities and the broader mission of the church. These events include music concerts, charity performances, and special giving campaigns aimed at raising funds for outreach missions, equipment, uniforms, and travel expenses for evangelism. Each fundraising effort is conducted prayerfully and transparently, inviting church members, partners, and the community to contribute toward spreading the Gospel through music. Through these events, we strengthen our unity, witness God’s provision, and expand our impact in fulfilling the Great Commission.

Chief/Head of the Choir

Secretary
