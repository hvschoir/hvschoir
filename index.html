    About Us

    Heaven Voice Singers (HVS) is a gospel choir focused on sharing the message of salvation through music, leading souls to Jesus and inspiring faith through every song.

    • Worship Services.
    • Gospel Concerts.
    • Outreach Programs

    Heaven Voice Singers (HVS) offers a wide range of services designed to spread the gospel and inspire faith through music. From leading worship services and organizing gospel concerts to outreach programs, evangelistic events, and workshops, we are committed to sharing the message of salvation. Our ministry extends to charitable initiatives, youth programs, and spiritual retreats, with a focus on reaching communities, supporting church planting, and providing healing through music. Through every performance and service, we aim to draw souls closer to Jesus and impact lives for His glory..
