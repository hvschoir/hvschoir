    About Us

    Heaven Voice Singers (HVS) is a gospel choir dedicated to spreading the gospel and inspiring f aith through music. We lead worship services, organize gospel concerts, and engage in outreach and evangelistic events. Our ministry also includes workshops, youth programs, charity work, and spiritual retreats—aiming to reach communities, support church planting, and bring healing through music. Every performance is an opportunity to draw souls closer to Jesus and glorify His name.

    • Worship Services – Leading praise in churches and revivals.
    • Gospel Concerts – Hosting spirit-filled musical events.
    • Outreach & Impact – Evangelism, youth programs, and charity.
