Tomas Magese

Chief/Head of the Choir

The Chairman of HVS Choir leads the choir’s activities, guides its direction, oversees events and fundraising, and supports the spiritual and musical growth of its members, ensuring alignment with the mission of AICT Tanzania.

Isack Bethuel Hanje

Secretary

The Choir Secretary is responsible for managing administrative tasks, coordinating rehearsals and events, and maintaining communication within the choir.

Ben Mpanduji

Music Director

The Music Director of HVS Choir is responsible for selecting and arranging music, leading rehearsals, and ensuring the choir’s performances are musically excellent. They guide vocal techniques, work with choir members to improve their skills, and ensure that each performance aligns with the spiritual and musical goals of the choir.

Happiness Paul Ndutu

Accountant

The accountant of HVS Choir plays a vital role in managing the choir's finances—handling income and expenses, maintaining accurate records, and ensuring transparency. Their work supports the smooth operation of the choir and helps build trust within the team and with supporters. .

Naomy Zunzu

Community Programs Officer

To develop and manage community-based initiatives that promote the HVS Choir’s mission, strengthen community relationships, and create opportunities for outreach, engagement, and impact through music.

Gladness Mussa

Wardrobe Coordinator

The Wardrobe Manager oversees the selection, organization, and maintenance of the choir’s outfits, ensuring they are appropriate, clean, and ready for performances.
