Chief/Head of the Choir

The Chairman of HVS Choir leads the choir’s activities, guides its direction, oversees events and fundraising, and supports the spiritual and musical growth of its members, ensuring alignment with the mission of AICT Tanzania.

Isack Bethuel Hanje

Secretary

The Choir Secretary is responsible for managing administrative tasks, coordinating rehearsals and events, and maintaining communication within the choir.

Ben Mpanduji

Music Director

The Music Director of HVS Choir is responsible for selecting and arranging music, leading rehearsals, and ensuring the choir’s performances are musically excellent. They guide vocal techniques, work with choir members to improve their skills, and ensure that each performance aligns with the spiritual and musical goals of the choir.
